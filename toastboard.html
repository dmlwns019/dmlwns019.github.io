Toastboard

Toastboard is a smart modular breadboard, microcontroller, and component system aimed at helping primary school kids understand electronic engineering in a visual way. Like puzzle pieces, Toastboard modules click together to create breadboard-like systems. One Toastboard module contains a microcontroller and an RGB LED allowing signals to be intuitively communicated through colors. Special components are also designed, such as servo motors, LEDs, sensors, and relays to aid children in learning electrical engineering.
